The Silent Observer

The Spectral Environment Tracker (S.E.T.) is designed for investigators who require actionable data over random noise. Unlike standard motion sensors that can be triggered by thermal drafts or insects, the S.E.T. utilizes a precision Ultrasonic Sensor to create a physical "tripwire."


By constantly measuring the distance to the nearest object (such as a hallway wall or door), the device establishes a baseline. If that distance changes—indicating an unseen mass has entered the space—the S.E.T. alerts you instantly. It is the unblinking eye for your investigation.

Technical Specifications

Ultrasonic Sensor

Advanced distance measuring technology that detects physical movement and unseen anomalies in total darkness.

8-Hour Battery Life

Equipped with a high-capacity power source designed for long-duration investigations without interruption.

Custom Firmware

Proprietary code written by Spectral Labs to eliminate false positives and ensure data integrity.
