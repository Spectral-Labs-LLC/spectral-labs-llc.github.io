The Silent Observer

The Spectral Environment Tracker (S.E.T.) is designed for investigators who require actionable data over random noise. Unlike standard motion sensors that can be triggered by thermal drafts or insects, the S.E.T. utilizes a precision Ultrasonic Sensor to create a physical "tripwire."


By constantly measuring the distance to the nearest object (such as a hallway wall or door), the device establishes a baseline. If that distance changes—indicating an unseen mass has entered the space—the S.E.T. alerts you instantly via the OLED display.

Technical Specifications

Ultrasonic Sensor
