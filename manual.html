Quick Start Guide

  1. Power On: Slide the toggle switch on the side of the unit to the "ON" position.
  2. Calibration: Place the device on a stable surface facing the area you wish to monitor. Do not move the device for 5 seconds while the sensor calibrates to the environment.
  3. Active Mode: Once the OLED display shows "ARMED," the tripwire is active.
  4. Alert: If the distance measurement changes drastically, the screen will flash and record the event.

Troubleshooting

Screen is blank?

Ensure the battery is fully charged via the USB-C port. Check that the power switch is fully engaged.

False Positives?

Ensure there are no moving objects (curtains, pets) directly in front of the ultrasonic sensor. The device works best in still environments.

Safety & Warnings

BATTERY WARNING: This device contains a rechargeable Lithium-Ion battery. Do not puncture, crush, or expose to extreme heat or fire. Do not leave charging unattended overnight.
