S.E.T. Device (Model v1.0)

Quick Start Guide

  1. Power On: Slide the toggle switch on the side to "ON".
  2. Calibrate: Place on a stable surface. Do not move for 5 seconds.
  3. Active: When OLED shows "ARMED," the tripwire is set.

Troubleshooting

Screen Blank? Charge via USB-C for 2 hours.

False Alarms? Ensure no thermal drafts or moving curtains are in the sensor path.

BATTERY WARNING: Contains Lithium-Ion. Do not puncture or incinerate.
