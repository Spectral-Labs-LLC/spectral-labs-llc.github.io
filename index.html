Our Mission

Spectral Labs LLC was founded with a single goal: to bridge the gap between expensive, inaccessible equipment and the investigators who need it most.


We believe that reliable data is the key to understanding the unknown. That’s why we design custom firmware, print our own rugged housings, and field-test every prototype in real haunted locations.
